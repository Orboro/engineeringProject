The Miracle of Mindfulness 
Led by Dr. Vanessa Miller-Curtis

Thursday, April 21 through Thursday, May 26, 2022
Time: 11:30am to 1:00pm (CST)

Thich Nhat Hanh said, “mindfulness is the capacity to be here, to witness deeply everything that happens in the present moment.” This course is about learning to apply mindfulness to our everyday lives through the teachings in The Miracle of Mindfulness. We will practice together, sit together, and reflect on and embrace the changes mindfulness can bring.

The class will meet every Thursday for 1.5 hours a week for 6 weeks. 

Cost $195.00
Book: The Miracle of Mindfulness by Thich Nhat Hanh
  -Participants purchase their own book
  -Electronic versions of this book may be available for free online

Vanessa Miller-Curtis, Ph.D. has over 12 years of experience in a variety of settings. Vanessa has spent several years developing and leading educational programs in the areas of Mindfulness, Shame and Guilt, and Contemplative Psychology in prison settings, outpatient clinics, and brain injury recovery and support groups. The effectiveness of therapeutic and educational programming for anyone on their overall well-being and its use as a tool to address anger, anxiety and other mental health issues is at the core of this work. Therapeutic educational programs pertain to psychological health and well-being. The importance of therapeutic and educational programming for any human being cannot adequately be expressed. For some, this may be the first opportunity to address a lifetime of maladaptive behaviors.
